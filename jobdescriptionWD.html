Position Titile : Front End/Web Developer

If you are a passionate and skilled Front End Developer looking for an exciting and challenging career opportunity, you can't miss this great position at our IT service business client who based in Melbourne.
In this role, you will have the opportunity to work on innovative and diverse projects, collaborate with talented professionals, and continuously learn and grow your skills. They value creativity, teamwork, and continuous improvement, and provide a supportive and inclusive environment where you can thrive and achieve your career goals. If you are eager to take your career to the next level and be part of a dynamic and rewarding team, please read on!

Responsibilities:

  1. Develop and maintain high-quality web applications using HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, React, VueJS, and NodeJS.
  2. Collaborate with designers, developers, and other team members to implement user-friendly interfaces and optimize website performance.
  3. Write clean, reusable, and maintainable code that meets industry standards and best practices.
  4. Conduct thorough unit testing and debugging to ensure the quality of the deliverables.
  5. Work with designers, such as graphic designers, to determine the site's layout.
  6. Continuously learn and stay up-to-date with the latest technologies, tools, and trends in web development.
  7. Working closely with the UX/UI designers.

Requirements:

  1. Working closely with the UX/UI designers.
  2. Proficient in HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, React, VueJS, and NodeJS.
  3. Experience with front end development tools such as Webpack, Babel, and NPM.
  4. Understanding of responsive web design, accessibility, and SEO best practices.
  5. Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment and communicate effectively.
  6. Passionate about delivering high-quality products and eager to learn and grow in a dynamic and challenging environment.
  7. Experience with Webpack or any external APIs.
  8. Experience on unit testing.
  9. Experience with building CI/CD pipeline.
  10. Applicants must be holding full Australian working rights.
