Position Titile : Web Developer

The following are the common tasks a web developer is expected to perform:

  1. Meet with clients to discuss their website design and requirements.
  2. Create and test apps for the website.
  3. Code for the site using HTML or XML and/or other programming languages.
  4. Engage with members of their team to ascertain the kind of information the site should contain.
  5. Work with designers, such as graphic designers, to determine the site's layout.
  6. Integrate audio, video, and graphics into the site.
  7. Quality-test and troubleshoot the website and/or its applications in various browsers.

Outside the realm of their actual work, web developers are expected to stay updated with market trends and learn new programming languages, frameworks, and technologies.

Salary Range: $60,000 - $80,000 per year
