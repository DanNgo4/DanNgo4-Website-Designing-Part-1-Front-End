Position Title : DevOps Engineer

As a DevOps Engineer you will be responsible for implementing application solutions both in the cloud as well as participating in technical research and development to enable continuing innovation within the DevOps space.

Specific responsibilities for this role include :

  1. Implement scalable, resilient, and secure solutions in the public cloud, especially in AWS.
  2. Participate in automation initiatives to streamline processes, improve efficiencies and reduce hosting cost.
  3. Work closely with Product Owner, Platform Team, Solution Architects and development teams for continuous improvement.
  4. Enhance and drive automation and "Infrastructure as Code" delivery.
  5. Deliver cloud projects in an Agile environment.
  6. Participating in technical discussion with existing & potential clients and internal teams,.
  7. Participate in research and development to deliver complex cloud-native solutions or on-premises.
  8. Ability to analyse and troubleshoot complex software and infrastructure issues, and develop tools/systems for task automation.
  9. BAU Support as needed for critical and escalated issue.
  10. Responsible for managing and upgrading DevOps toolsets.
  11. Maintaining 100% automation coverage of core Insight build and deploy using cloud-native services and containers.

Requirements :

  1. At least 3 to 5 years' experience working with cloud services like AWS.
  2. Solid experience in designing and implementing complex DevOps solutions
  3. Experience within cloud hosted environments such as Amazon AWS and Google GCP cloud, AWS Lamda and AWS CloudFormation.
  4. A good AWS DevOps development background

Salary Range:

Up to AU$165,000 per Annum
